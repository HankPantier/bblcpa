## Your first step toward stress-free accounting starts here

Getting started with BBL is simple, and there are no wrong answers here. Tell us where your business stands today, and we will take it from there. No spreadsheets to clean up first. No judgment about that shoebox of receipts. Since 1950, we have helped Southeast Texas physicians, restaurant owners, farmers, and small business owners hand off the accounting headaches and get back to running their businesses. The first conversation costs you nothing but a few minutes, and it sets the tone for everything that follows: clear, calm, and built around your needs.

## Tell us a little about your business

Think of this as a conversation starter, not an application. Tell us your name, what kind of business you run, and the one accounting problem keeping you up at night. Maybe payroll eats your Fridays. Maybe tax season sneaks up every year. Whatever it is, share it, and we will reach out to talk through how BBL can lighten the load. No commitment, no pressure, just a starting point.

[Schedule a consultation](/contact)

<!-- block: process-steps | variant: vertical -->
## Here is what happens after you reach out

### We start with a real conversation
Your first consultation is a chance for us to listen. You tell us about your business, your pain points, and what you want off your plate. We ask questions. Nobody pulls out a contract.

### We review what you actually need
Next, we look at where your books stand and what your business requires, from monthly bookkeeping to year-round tax planning. If your accounting system needs a cleanup before we begin, we will say so plainly.

### We match you to the right bundle
Based on that review, we recommend the specific services that fit your business, whether that is payroll alone or full outsourced accounting with CFO-level oversight. You only pay for what you use.

### We hand you a fixed-fee proposal
Here is the part business owners love: one flat monthly fee, written out in advance. You see exactly what is included before you say yes. No hourly surprises, no end-of-month invoices that make you wince.

### We set up your secure platform
Once you are on board, we get you onto our secure online platform so you can see your financials in real time, from anywhere. From there, the busywork becomes ours, and your numbers become clear.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Get started with BBL

**Q: How do I get started with BBL in Port Arthur, TX?**

**Q: Will I know the cost before I commit?**
A: Yes. After reviewing your needs, BBL gives you a written fixed monthly fee proposal that spells out exactly what is included. There are no hourly surprises and no end-of-month invoices you did not expect. You see the full price before you say yes.

**Q: What services can BBL bundle into one monthly fee?**
