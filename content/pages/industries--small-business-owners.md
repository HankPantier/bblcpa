## Running a small business is hard enough without the bookkeeping headaches

Running a restaurant in Port Arthur or a dental practice in Beaumont leaves little time for reconciling bank statements or chasing a missing invoice. Most small business owners we meet are reacting to their finances instead of managing them: a tax bill shows up without warning, a payroll glitch eats an afternoon, and nobody can say with certainty what the business actually made last month.

Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore takes that weight off your desk. We handle the bookkeeping, payroll, and tax work so you can spend your time on customers, patients, or crops instead of spreadsheets. You started this business to do the work you're good at. Let us handle the numbers behind it.

## More than 70 years of serving Southeast Texas small businesses

Founded in 1950, our firm has worked out of Port Arthur for more than seven decades, long enough to have prepared taxes for three generations of the same family business and to have walked agriculture operations in Jefferson County through hurricane seasons and commodity swings. We've watched local restaurants change hands, dental practices expand, and new businesses open their doors for the first time, often with our help.

That history is institutional memory, and it's useful to you in a practical way. A large national firm rotates you through a revolving cast of associates who don't know Southeast Texas. Here, the people on your account know the local economy, the local tax authorities, and likely a few of your competitors down the street. That's not a sales pitch, it's just how a firm built since 1950 ends up operating.

## Tax support that works all year, not just in April

A lot of small business owners only hear from their accountant during tax season, which means the planning that would have lowered the bill already happened months ago, without them. We work the calendar differently. Tax planning happens in the spring, the summer, and the fall, so by the time a return is filed, there are no surprises left to find.

If the IRS sends a notice anyway, Audit Protection puts a CPA between you and the correspondence. We handle the letters, the documentation, and the proceedings directly, at no extra charge beyond your monthly fee. That's the difference between a firm that disappears after April 15 and one that stays on the phone with the IRS in October if it needs to.

<!-- block: checklist-section | variant: with-image | image: cfo-advisory-business-owner.jpg | alt: "Small business owner reviewing financial reports with an advisor" | query: "business advisor reviewing financial reports" -->
## CFO-level guidance without the CFO-level cost

A full-time CFO costs more than most small businesses can justify, but the decisions a CFO handles, cash flow, growth timing, entity structure, retirement planning, still have to get made. Business Management Services puts that level of financial oversight on your team at a fraction of the cost of a hire.

In practice, that means:

- Cash flow forecasting that flags a problem before it becomes an emergency
- Growth planning built on your actual numbers, not a guess
- Entity structure review through Entity Type Analysis, so you're not overpaying in taxes because of how the business is set up
- Retirement plan options compared side by side, so you pick the one that fits a small business budget

It's the strategic side of accounting, the part most bookkeeping services never touch.

## Starting a business in Southeast Texas? We help you build it right from day one

The choices you make in the first few months, entity type, accounting setup, how payroll gets structured, are expensive to undo later. Business Foundation Services and Entity Type Analysis walk new owners through those decisions before they're locked in, with the tax and liability consequences spelled out in plain terms.

We're not just here to file your paperwork. We're here to make sure the foundation under the business can hold the weight of growth. If you're opening your doors for the first time, our [new business owners](/industries/new-business-owners) page covers what that looks like in more detail.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Accounting and advisory services for small business owners

**Q: What does outsourced accounting cost for a small business in Port Arthur?**
A: Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore bundles bookkeeping, payroll, tax, and business management into one fixed monthly fee, so there's no per-task billing or surprise invoices. The exact fee depends on your business size and service needs, which we walk through during a consultation.

**Q: Do you only work with businesses in Port Arthur, TX?**
A: Our office is based in Port Arthur, but we serve small business owners, physicians, dentists, restaurants, and agriculture operations throughout Southeast Texas. Services are delivered through a secure online platform, so location isn't a barrier to working together.

**Q: What happens if my business gets an IRS notice?**
A: Audit Protection is included in our service model and covers IRS notices, letters, and proceedings directly. A CPA handles the correspondence on your behalf rather than leaving you to interpret it alone or pay extra for representation.

**Q: Can you help me choose the right business structure before I open?**
A: Yes. Entity Type Analysis compares LLC, S-corp, and other structures against your specific tax and liability situation, and Business Foundation Services supports the broader startup setup, including accounting systems and payroll structure from day one.
