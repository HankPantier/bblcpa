## What happens after you reach out

Submit the form below or call our office, and here's what happens next. You'll hear from a member of our team within one business day, usually sooner. We'll set up a short call, fifteen to twenty minutes, to understand your business, your industry, and what's eating up your time. We'll walk you through fixed monthly fee options for accounting, payroll, or tax support, with no surprise invoices and no pressure to sign anything on the spot.

If you've got a tax notice sitting on your desk or need IRS representation, tell us up front. We move faster on those. Once you're a client, everything runs through our secure online platform, so you get real-time visibility into your numbers from day one.
