## Tax and financial forms for individuals and businesses

Knowing which form applies is half the battle. Here are the ones clients in Southeast Texas reach for most, labeled in plain English so you don't second-guess yourself.

- **Form W-4**, Tell your employer how much federal tax to withhold from each paycheck.
- **Form W-9**, Share your taxpayer ID with a business that needs to issue you a 1099.
- **Form 1040**, The individual federal income tax return for personal filers.
- **Form 1099-NEC**, Report payments of $600 or more made to contractors and freelancers.
- **Form SS-4**, Apply for an Employer Identification Number when you start a business.
- **Texas Sales and Use Tax Return**, File and remit sales tax collected from your customers.
- **Form 941**, Report quarterly payroll taxes withheld from employee wages.

Not sure whether you need a W-9 or a 1099? That's exactly the kind of question our [tax](https://www.bblcpa.com/what-we-do/tax) and [payroll](https://www.bblcpa.com/what-we-do/payroll) teams answer year-round.

## Government and agency links for common tasks

We've grouped the official links around what you actually came here to do, so you spend less time digging through menus.

### Check your federal refund
icon: DollarSign

Use the IRS "Where's My Refund?" tool to track your federal return status. Prefer to watch it with us? Try our [refund tracker](https://www.bblcpa.com/resources/refund-tracker).

### Make a federal tax payment
icon: CreditCard

IRS Direct Pay lets you send a payment straight from your bank account for individual taxes, estimated payments, and balances due.

### Handle Texas sales tax
icon: Receipt

The Texas Comptroller's Webfile system is where you register, file, and pay state sales and use tax for your business.

### Register a Texas business
icon: Building

The Texas Secretary of State handles entity formation and filings. Pair it with our [entity type analysis](https://www.bblcpa.com/what-we-do/entity-type-analysis) before you choose a structure.

### Manage Social Security
icon: UserCheck

The Social Security Administration site covers benefit estimates, replacement cards, and wage reporting for you and your employees.

### Look up an EIN
icon: FileText

Apply for or confirm your Employer Identification Number directly through the IRS online application.
