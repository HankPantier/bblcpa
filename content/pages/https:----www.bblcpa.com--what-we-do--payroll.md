## Full-service payroll so you can get back to running your business

Payroll is one of those jobs that never quite leaves your desk. Deadlines come whether you are ready or not, tax rules shift, and one missed filing can turn into a penalty letter. That is the part we take off your plate completely.

This is a full handoff, not another software login you have to babysit. We run your payroll through a secure online platform, handle the filings, and keep you compliant so you are never the one chasing a deadline. You stay focused on patients, customers, crews, or whatever actually grows your business.

Business owners across Southeast Texas already trust us to keep their people paid accurately and on time. You get the same steady hand, the same plain answers, and a payroll process that simply works in the background while you run the show.

## Everything included in your payroll service

Think of this as your whole payroll department, handled by people who do it every day. Here is what we manage for you:

- **Scheduled payroll processing** on the pay cycle you choose, weekly, biweekly, semimonthly, or monthly.
- **Employee setup** for every new hire, including the paperwork and details that keep things compliant from day one.
- **Salary and wage changes** processed cleanly, so raises, bonuses, and rate adjustments show up correctly.
- **Payroll tax filings** prepared and submitted on schedule, federal and state, so deadlines never sneak up on you.
- **Direct deposit** that puts pay in your team's accounts on time, every time.
- **Compliance reporting** that keeps your records straight and audit-ready.

No single piece of this should land back on your desk. We handle the routine runs and the odd situations, like a mid-cycle hire or a correction that needs to happen fast. When a question comes up, you talk to a real person who already knows your account, not a help-line script. The goal is simple: every detail covered, nothing for you to remember.

## No surprise bills, no hidden fees

Most people brace themselves before opening an invoice from an accounting firm. We took that worry out entirely. Your payroll service is part of a fixed monthly fee, so you know the exact number before the month begins and you see the same number when it ends.

No per-run charges. No add-ons that show up after the fact. No clock running every time you call with a question. One predictable amount, bundled with the rest of your service, so payroll fits your budget instead of surprising it.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Payroll services | Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore CPAs & Consultants

**Q: What does your full-service payroll include?**
A: We handle scheduled payroll processing, new employee setup, salary and wage changes, federal and state payroll tax filings, direct deposit, and compliance reporting. It all runs through a secure online platform, so you never have to track deadlines or file paperwork yourself.

**Q: How much does payroll cost with BBL?**
A: Payroll is part of a fixed monthly fee bundled with your other services. You know the exact amount before the month begins, with no per-run charges, no hidden add-ons, and no clock running when you call with a question. The price stays predictable.

**Q: Can I see my payroll information whenever I need it?**
A: Yes. Our secure online platform gives you real-time visibility into your payroll activity. Log in any time to see what was paid, when it went out, and where your numbers stand, without calling or waiting for someone to email you a report.

**Q: Do you handle payroll for restaurants and seasonal businesses?**
A: We do. We have managed tipped wages for restaurants, seasonal crews for agriculture, and multi-provider pay structures for medical and dental practices across Southeast Texas for over 70 years. The wrinkles in your industry are familiar territory for our team.

**Q: Where is BBL located?**
A: Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore CPAs & Consultants is based in Port Arthur, Texas, and has served small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals throughout Southeast Texas since 1950. We provide year-round payroll, tax, and accounting support, not just end-of-year help.
