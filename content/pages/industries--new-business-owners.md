## Getting your entity structure right from the start saves money later

The entity you choose shapes how much tax you pay, how you get paid, and how exposed your personal assets are if something goes wrong. A sole proprietorship is simple to start, but it offers no liability protection. An S-Corp can save real money on self-employment tax once your profit climbs, but it comes with payroll requirements and stricter rules. An LLC sits somewhere in between with flexibility on how it is taxed.

Pick the wrong structure and you can overpay for years or scramble to restructure mid-stream, which is rarely cheap or fun. Our Entity Type Analysis service looks at your expected income, your growth plans, and your appetite for paperwork, then recommends the structure that fits. We also handle the state filings so the entity you chose is the entity that actually exists on paper. Getting this decision right early is one of the quietest, most valuable things a new owner can do.
