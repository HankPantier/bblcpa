## Your books should work for you, not against you

A bookkeeping system that fights you every month costs more than time. Misclassified expenses, bank feeds that don't match, and a chart of accounts built for someone else's business turn tax season into a scramble and make it nearly impossible to know where you actually stand. Across Port Arthur and the rest of Southeast Texas, we see the same pattern again and again: a small business owner inherits a setup from a previous bookkeeper, a software default, or plain trial and error, and nobody ever goes back to fix it. Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore CPAs & Consultants builds and repairs accounting systems so the numbers on your screen are the numbers that matter, set up once, configured correctly, and ready to support real decisions.

<!-- block: checklist-section | variant: with-image | image: accounting-system-cleanup.jpg | alt: "CPA configuring chart of accounts on a desktop computer" | query: "accountant setting up bookkeeping software" -->
## What our accounting system setup service includes

Some businesses need a system built from the ground up. Others have years of tangled data that needs sorting before it's useful for anything, including your taxes. Either way, this is hands-on configuration work, not a one-hour consultation followed by a PDF.

- New system builds in QuickBooks, Xero, or your platform of choice, configured around how your business actually operates
- Cleanup of existing files, including reclassifying transactions, reconciling old accounts, and fixing duplicate entries
- Chart of accounts structured for your industry and your entity type, so reports actually mean something
- Integration checks across bank feeds, payroll, point-of-sale, and merchant processing accounts
- Workflow configuration so recurring transactions, invoices, and bills route to the right place automatically
- Documentation and a walkthrough for your team, so the system stays clean after we hand it back

If you're also restructuring how the business itself is set up, our [entity type analysis](https://www.bblcpa.com/what-we-do/entity-type-analysis) service pairs naturally with a system rebuild.

## A clean system pays for itself from day one

A properly built system doesn't just look better. It changes how fast everything downstream moves. Bookkeeping takes less time because transactions land where they belong instead of in a miscellaneous bucket. Financial statements come out accurate the first time, which means fewer surprises when tax season hits. And because the setup connects directly to our secure online platform, you get real-time visibility into cash position, profit, and expenses without waiting on a monthly report.

This is also where the fixed monthly fee model pays off. Setup connects straight into our [outsourced accounting](https://www.bblcpa.com/what-we-do/outsourced-accounting) service, so you're not paying separately every time something needs adjusting. One bundled fee, no billing surprises, and a system built to support it from the start.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Accounting system setup | Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore CPAs & Consultants

**Q: How long does accounting system setup take?**
A: Timelines depend on whether you need a new build or a cleanup of existing books. A straightforward new system can be configured in a few weeks, while a multi-year cleanup with reconciliation issues may take longer. We scope the work upfront so there's no guessing.

**Q: Do you work with QuickBooks and other platforms?**
A: Yes. We set up and clean up QuickBooks, Xero, and other common platforms, configuring them around your industry, entity type, and existing integrations like payroll and point-of-sale systems so everything flows correctly from day one.

**Q: Is accounting system setup a one-time service?**
A: It can be, but most clients pair it with our ongoing outsourced accounting or bookkeeping service so the same team maintaining your books also built the system. That keeps everything consistent and avoids the system drifting back into disorder.

**Q: What if my books are a mess already?**
A: That's the most common starting point. We reconcile old accounts, reclassify transactions, and rebuild the chart of accounts from whatever state your records are in now, whether that's years of tangled files or a system nobody's touched since it was installed.
