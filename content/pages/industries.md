## Accounting built around how your industry works

No two industries handle money the same way. A restaurant counts cash flow by the shift; a farm counts it by the season; a dental practice carries overhead a freelancer never thinks about. A one-size accounting package ignores all of that. Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore shapes its fixed monthly fee bundle around the real pressures your field deals with, the compliance rules you answer to, and the cash flow pattern your year actually follows. Below are the industries we know best across Southeast Texas, and how that knowledge shows up in the work.

<!-- block: content-split | variant: image-right | image: physician-finances.jpg | alt: "Physician reviewing practice financial reports with an accountant at a desk" | query: "doctor reviewing financial documents office" -->
## Accounting and tax support for physicians

Between patient panels, practice overhead, and income that arrives through several different channels, your finances rarely fit a simple template. You went to medical school, not accounting school, and the back office shouldn't eat your evenings. Year-round [tax planning](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/outsourced-accounting/tax) keeps your liability from becoming a March surprise, so a decision you make in June can lower what you owe in April. [Payroll](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/outsourced-accounting/payroll) for your clinical and admin staff runs through a secure online platform, off your plate entirely. And our [business management services](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/business-management-services) give you CFO-level guidance, regular financial reporting, and a clear read on where the practice stands, at a fraction of the cost of a full-time hire. The fixed monthly fee means no surprise invoice after a long phone call. See how we support [physicians](/industries/physicians) across Southeast Texas.

<!-- block: content-split | variant: image-left | image: restaurant-owner-books.jpg | alt: "Restaurant owner checking sales figures on a tablet near the kitchen" | query: "restaurant owner reviewing finances kitchen" -->
## Financial guidance for restaurant owners

Margins in food service are thin enough that a few points of food cost or one overstaffed Saturday can erase a good week. Cash comes in daily, vendors and payroll go out constantly, and the picture changes fast. Our [outsourced accounting](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/outsourced-accounting) bundles bookkeeping, payroll, and reporting into one fixed monthly fee, so labor and food costs stay visible while you run the line. The secure online platform shows real-time numbers, no waiting until month-end to learn a location slipped. Tip reporting, multi-shift payroll, and sales tax filings get handled by a seasoned team instead of landing on your desk at midnight. Should the IRS send a notice, [audit protection](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/outsourced-accounting/audit-protection) puts an expert between you and the paperwork. Predictable billing, clear numbers, one less thing to manage during the dinner rush. Explore how we serve [restaurants](/industries/restaurants).

<!-- block: content-split | variant: image-right | image: dental-practice-owner.jpg | alt: "Dentist and practice manager looking over business paperwork in an office" | query: "dentist business owner office paperwork" -->
## Tax and accounting services for dental practices

Running a dental practice means being two people at once: the clinician in the chair and the owner watching the books. The business side decides how much of your production you keep. Choosing the right structure matters here, so our [entity type analysis](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/business-foundation-services/entity-type-analysis) compares S-corp, LLC, and other options against your numbers and the tax that follows. A practice builds wealth fastest when the retirement plan fits, which is why our [retirement plan analysis](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/business-foundation-services/retirement-plan-analysis) weighs SEP, SIMPLE, and 401(k) routes against your goals. Day to day, [outsourced accounting](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/outsourced-accounting) keeps the books clean, payroll current, and compliance handled through a secure platform, all under one fixed monthly fee. The result is a practice that pays you well now and sets you up for later. See how we work with [dentists](/industries/dentists) in Port Arthur and beyond.

## Accounting that understands the rhythm of agriculture

Farm income doesn't arrive in twelve equal pieces. It comes with the harvest, the herd, or the contract, and the months in between still have bills. Equipment depreciation, commodity price swings, and seasonal labor all shape how a farm or ranch should plan its taxes and cash. Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore has worked with Southeast Texas businesses since 1950, so the rhythm of an agricultural year is familiar ground. The fixed monthly fee gives you a steady, predictable cost even when revenue is anything but, and the secure online platform keeps your numbers current between seasons. Strategic tax planning times equipment purchases and income recognition to your advantage rather than the calendar's. Steady support through unpredictable seasons is the whole point. See how we serve [agriculture](/industries/agriculture) businesses across Southeast Texas.

## Support for small business owners and new business owners

Two paths lead here, and both end with cleaner books. Established owners often reach a point where bookkeeping, payroll, and tax filings take more time than the work that brings in revenue, and bundling all of it into one fixed monthly fee hands those hours back. New owners face a different list: which entity to form, how to set up the books, what the first year of compliance looks like. Our [business foundation services](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/business-foundation-services) help you launch with a structure and a system that hold up, while [entity type analysis](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/business-foundation-services/entity-type-analysis) and [accounting system setup](https://www.bblcpa.com/services/business-foundation-services/accounting-system-setup) handle the technical decisions early, before they turn into cleanup projects. Either way, you get a seasoned team and real-time visibility instead of a shoebox of receipts. Explore support for [small business owners](/industries/small-business-owners) and [new business owners](/industries/new-business-owners).
