## Your own accounting department, without the overhead

Running a business in Port Arthur means wearing every hat: owner, manager, and sometimes bookkeeper at 11 p.m. Outsourced accounting from Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore hands that last job to a team that's been doing it since 1950. We reconcile your accounts, manage your books, and keep your financial records current every month, without you hiring a single employee or learning a new piece of software.

Think of it as adding a full accounting department to your business, minus the payroll, the office space, and the turnover. Transactions get classified. Statements get prepared on schedule. Your questions get answered by a real person who already knows your business, not a call center reading from a script. You focus on patients, plates, crops, or whatever it is that pays your bills.

## Everything bundled into one fixed monthly fee

Traditional accounting bills by the hour, which means every phone call, every question, and every small fix can show up as a line item you didn't see coming. BBL works differently. One flat monthly fee covers everything in your plan, so you can budget accounting costs the same way you budget rent or utilities.

Depending on the plan you choose, your fixed fee can include:

- Bank, credit card, and electronic transaction reconciliation
- Monthly financial statements and budget reports
- Full-service payroll processing, tax filings, and W-2 preparation
- Bill pay, expense tracking, and 1099 preparation for contractors
- Year-round business tax planning and preparation
- General business consulting and a quarterly review of your key numbers

No surprise invoices show up at month's end. No guessing what a "quick question" will cost you this time. You know the number before the month starts, and that number doesn't move because your accountant had a busy week. It's the kind of predictability most small business owners in Southeast Texas don't expect from a CPA firm, until they switch.

## Real-time financial visibility through a secure online platform

You shouldn't have to call your accountant to find out how your business is doing. Every BBL outsourced accounting client gets access to a secure online platform where your books, statements, and reports live and update continuously. Log in from your office, your truck, or your kitchen table, and see where things stand right now, not where they stood last quarter.

The same platform lets you upload documents, message our team, and pull paid-bill records without digging through a filing cabinet. We're looking at the same numbers you are, which means when you ask a question, you get an answer instead of a callback tomorrow. No new portal to figure out. Just a clear, current picture of your business, available whenever you need it.

## Who this service is built for

Outsourced accounting works best for business owners who'd rather spend their hours on the work only they can do. Across Southeast Texas, that covers a specific list of professions BBL has worked with for decades.

### Physicians
icon: Stethoscope

Practice overhead, insurance reimbursement timing, and staff payroll eat into hours you'd rather spend with patients. We keep the books current and payroll compliant so the practice runs on schedule, not on guesswork.

### Dentists
icon: HeartPulse

Equipment financing, hygienist payroll, and managing two or three locations leave little time for an evening with QuickBooks. That's the work we handle every month, on the same platform, at the same fixed fee.

### Restaurants
icon: Receipt

Thin margins, tip reporting, and weekly vendor payments make restaurant books unforgiving of mistakes. Outsourced accounting keeps reconciliation current, so a cash-flow problem shows up in week one, not month three.

### Agriculture
icon: Wallet

Seasonal income, equipment depreciation, and land-related tax questions don't fit a generic bookkeeping template. BBL has worked with Southeast Texas agriculture operations long enough to know the difference between a good year and a good year on paper.

### New and growing small business owners
icon: TrendingUp

You started the business to do the work, not to become an accountant. Outsourced accounting gives new owners a financial foundation and gives growing owners room to scale, without adding back-office headcount.
