## Plain-language guides to help you run a smarter business

This library was built for business owners, not accountants. The guides, magazines, and news briefs here answer the money questions you actually run into: how to read a financial statement, when to rethink your entity type, what a new tax rule means for your payroll. Whether you run a clinic in Port Arthur, a restaurant down the road, a dental practice, a farm, or a startup anywhere in Southeast Texas, the goal is the same. Read something here, walk away knowing your next move, and skip the jargon. No login required to browse.

## Advantage Magazine, your quarterly financial read

Four times a year, Advantage Magazine lands with a mix of tax updates, small business strategy, and money topics you can act on. One issue might cover retirement plan choices for a growing practice. The next might break down cash-flow habits that keep restaurants and farms steady through a slow season. It is written for owners, not specialists, so the takeaways are concrete.

The magazine is also proof of something we believe: tax season is one month, but your financial questions run year-round. Advantage exists to keep useful information in front of you in March, July, and every month between. Browse the current issue and the archive in the [resources library](https://www.bblcpa.com/resources/resources-library).

## Frequently Asked Questions About Resources library | Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore CPAs & Consultants

**Q: Who writes the content in the resources library?**
