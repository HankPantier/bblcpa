## One flat monthly fee covers everything you need

Most accounting bills feel like a surprise party nobody wanted. A few extra emails, a quick phone call, a "while we're at it" project, and suddenly the invoice has doubled. BBL's Outsourced Accounting service runs differently: one fixed monthly fee, set in advance, that bundles bookkeeping, payroll, tax preparation, and year-round support into a single predictable payment.

That means no per-question billing, no surprise add-ons when tax season gets complicated, and no guessing what next month's invoice will look like. You get a full back-office accounting team without negotiating a new rate every time your business needs something.

This matters most for small business owners juggling cash flow, because a flat fee is easy to budget for and easy to defend when a partner or lender asks where the money goes. It's accounting support that behaves the way a fixed cost should: stable, transparent, and the same number every month, whether it's a quiet January or a chaotic April.

- Bookkeeping and transaction classification handled monthly
- Full-service payroll processed through one secure system
- Tax planning and preparation included, not billed separately
- Year-round support, not just a rush in April

## Real-time financial visibility through a secure online platform

A lot of firms still operate on a once-a-quarter check-in: you send documents, wait, and find out where you stand weeks after the fact. BBL clients log into a secure online platform and see their financial picture as it exists right now, not as it existed last month.

That changes how you run a business. Payroll runs, tax documents, financial statements, and account activity are all in one place, available any time you need to check them, not just when a meeting is scheduled. If a lender asks for current financials or you want to know whether you can afford a new hire this quarter, the answer is a login away instead of a phone call and a wait.

It's the difference between being told what happened and actually watching it happen. For a small business owner in Port Arthur trying to make a decision on a Tuesday afternoon, that kind of access is the whole point.

## CFO-level guidance without the CFO-level cost

A full-time CFO is out of reach for most independent physicians, dental practices, and small business owners, and frankly it's overkill for a lot of them. But the financial leadership a CFO provides, regular reporting, cash flow forecasting, growth planning, isn't optional once a practice or business gets past a certain size.

BBL's Business Management Services fill that gap. You get the same level of financial oversight a CFO would provide: regular financial reviews, strategic guidance on growth and spending decisions, and someone who actually reads your numbers and tells you what they mean, at a fraction of what a full-time hire would cost.

This fits physicians and dentists especially well, since clinical training rarely includes business finance, but the practice still runs on margins, staffing costs, and overhead just like any other business. You don't need to add a six-figure salary to your payroll to get someone watching the financial side. You need the right guidance at the right scale, which is exactly what this service is built for.
