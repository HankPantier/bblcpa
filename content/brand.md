# About Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore CPAs & Consultants

## Identity

Brammer, Begnaud, & Lattimore CPAs & Consultants is a full-service accounting and consulting firm established in 1950, based in Port Arthur, Texas, with over seven decades serving small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals throughout Southeast Texas. The firm's tagline, "Let us guide you toward a healthy financial future," reflects its overall approach.

The firm is led by David L. Lattimore (Managing Partner, CPA) and Erik Angelle (Senior Partner, CPA), supported by an administrative team including Susan Worsham (Office Administrator), Angela Jennings (Administrative Assistant), and professional staff Carise Mills, Belinda Bourgeois, Nichole Mott, and Orgesa Vokshi.

The firm's tone is approachable, reassuring, client-friendly, conversational, and benefit-focused — using plain language and light wordplay to make accounting feel accessible and stress-free rather than intimidating.

## Positioning & Differentiation

The firm positions itself around removing the burden of complex accounting, payroll, and tax work from business owners and professionals so they can focus on running their operations. Its core differentiators are:

- Fixed monthly fee bundled services with no billing surprises
- A secure online platform giving clients real-time financial visibility
- Year-round tax planning and support, not just seasonal filing
- CFO-level business management guidance at a fraction of typical cost

Services span outsourced accounting, bookkeeping, payroll, business and personal tax preparation, audit protection and IRS representation, business management (CFO-level) services, and business foundation services for new companies (entity selection, accounting system setup, retirement plan analysis).
